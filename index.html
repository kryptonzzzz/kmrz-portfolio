01 — Profile

About me

I am Kim Joshua L. Razon, a Bachelor of Science in Meteorology graduate from Central Luzon State University, with hands-on training at PAGASA's Baguio Synoptic Station and BSU's Agromet Station.

My background spans atmospheric science, instrumentation, and technology — including web development, embedded systems, QGIS, and AI. I enjoy finding practical ways to make meteorological work simpler and more efficient, and I carry a deep respect for the science and the people who shaped it before me.
